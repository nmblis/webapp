Frequently Asked Questions

What in the world is a Nmblis?

Think of Nmblis as a better business card. Your Personal Billboard. All the same info you have on a business card, you can have on a Nmblis. Plus some - clickable email addresses, websites, phone numbers, social media links, your picture, etc...

But why?

Let's say you're an up and coming go-getter, who wears many hats, one title doesn't fit you, and you'd need multiple cards. Nmblis. Or say you just want to go green, and stop carrying a stack of business cards around. Nmblis. Or you're a business wanting to reduce your bottom line somehow someway. Nmblis.
